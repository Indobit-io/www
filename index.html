Why Indobit?

Indonesia needs a privacy-first identity solution that respects user autonomy while enabling secure digital interactions. Indobit leverages zero-knowledge proofs to verify identity attributes without exposing sensitive personal data.

Privacy by Design

Verify your identity without revealing unnecessary personal information. Zero-knowledge proofs ensure minimal data exposure.

Seamless Onboarding

Start with simple phone verification and gradually build your decentralized identity with additional claims.

Made for Indonesia

Designed specifically for Indonesia's digital ecosystem and regulatory requirements while maintaining global standards.

Project Milestones

Our roadmap outlines the phased development of Indobit from initial MVP to full decentralized protocol.

Current Phase Q3 2023 - Q1 2024

Phase 1 – MVP: ZK Phone Proof

Launch of our minimum viable product with phone number verification using zero-knowledge proofs. Users can prove ownership of an Indonesian phone number without revealing the actual number.

zk-SNARKs Mobile SDK Testnet
Q2 2024 - Q4 2024

Phase 2 – Expand Claim Types

Introduction of additional verification methods including government ID validation, social media attestations, and reputation-based claims while maintaining privacy.

ID Verification Social Proofs Mainnet
Q1 2025 - Q3 2025

Phase 3 – SDK, Dev Adoption, and Proof Aggregation

Release of developer tools and SDKs for easy integration. Implementation of proof aggregation to combine multiple attestations into a single efficient proof.

Developer Tools Proof Aggregation Partner Integrations
Q4 2025+

Phase 4 – Full zkID Protocol with DAO Governance

Transition to full decentralization with community governance. The protocol becomes self-sustaining with token incentives for validators and attestors.

DAO Governance Token Economy Full Decentralization
